Lindsay's Pixel Art Maker

Choose a grid size

Enter a value between 1 and 100 for both width and height. The default is 25x25.

Choose a technique

In Click Mode, click on the square to color it. Clicking again will clear the color. In Free Draw Mode, roll your mouse over the squares to draw.

Choose a color

Think of this step as dipping your brush in the paint. To change the color that you are painting with, just select a new color!

Need some help?

You can upload a background photo to guide your painting! Please provide the complete URL to a .jpg, .jpeg, or .png photo.
